In this work we develop and release Llama 2 a collection of pretrained and fine45;tuned large language models (LLMs) ranging in scale from 7 billion to 70 billion parameters. Our fine45;tuned LLMs called Llama 245;Chat are optimized for dialogue use cases. Our models outperform open45;source chat models on most benchmarks we tested and based on our human evaluations for helpfulness and safety may be a suitable substitute for closed45;source models. We provide a detailed description of our approach to fine45;tuning and safety improvements of Llama 245;Chat in order to enable the community to build on our work and contribute to the responsible development of LLMs.
