In this report the technical details of our submission to the EPIC45;Kitchens Action Anticipation Challenge 2021 are given. We developed a hierarchical attention model for action anticipation which leverages Transformer45;based attention mechanism to aggregate features across temporal dimension modalities symbiotic branches respectively. In terms of Mean Top45;5 Recall of action our submission with team name ICL45;SJTU achieved 13.3937; for overall testing set 10.0537; for unseen subsets and 11.8837; for tailed subsets. Additionally it is noteworthy that our submission ranked 1st in terms of verb class in all three (sub)sets.
